Wreck Camp Extended Orientation

Promotional Materials created with Adobe Suite

These flyers were created for counselor and participant recruitment for Georgia Tech Wreck Camp, an extended orientation program for incoming freshmen at Georgia Tech. They were all created using either Indesign, Photoshop, or both. They are a sample of many flyers, videos, websites, and other media tools that I created over the years. I have held the positions of crew member, Director of Participant Relations, Executive Director, and counselor over the years.
