The Home Depot

Forcasting Analytics and Oculus Rift

I worked on many projects throughout the Summer and Fall during my Internship, and some are not available to physically show. In the first picture, you can see a UI I worked on that introduced Teradata forecasting and analytics upload service to The Home Depot’s demand chain management web service. I wrote and implemented the DAO call and service logic, queried from Teradata tables using SQL, and the new function I added to the UI with Java and HTML is shown in the picture. The second picture displays my work on Leapmotion and Oculus Rift. The shelves and boxes were created to simulate a store, and the Information viewer simply showed box sizes upon hovering over an item when switching.
